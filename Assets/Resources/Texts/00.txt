1.1  End extreme poverty for all people around the world (currently, extreme poverty is defined as living on less than $ 1.25 per day)
1.2  Reducing the proportion of men, women and children of all ages living in all forms of poverty, according to national definitions, by at least half
1.3  Implement appropriate social protection systems and measures at the national level for all, including minimum levels (social protection), and achieve significant coverage of the poor and vulnerable
